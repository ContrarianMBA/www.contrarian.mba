An MBA is predominately a networking or career change exercise. Across the board, the curriculum is weak and outdated, which follows from most of academia (except cutting-edge criticism). The following Contrarian MBA curriculum introduces business principles, the economy, finance, political ramifications, and more through books by or about practitioners.
